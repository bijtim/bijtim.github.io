To Our Valued Customers,

Together, we find ourselves in an extraordinary situation with COVID-19. In the past several months, things have moved very quickly, and this pandemic is affecting all of our communities, our families, our small-businesses and our very way of life. We have faithfully been serving the community superior coffee, pizza and gelato/ ice cream for the past 10 years, and we are so happy to be able to serve our valued patrons again after a temporary pandemic closure. We hope you will continue to support small, local businesses, like Vovito, by enjoying our exquisite coffee and delicious sweet and savory snack at home or gifting it to family, friends, and your favorite Heroes! We promise it will make them SMILE! These are unprecedented times and we want to express our deepest gratitude to all those frontline workers who are serving our community and saving lives daily. If you would like to donate Vovito Coffee or a “HERO Combo” to them, we will happily ship the coffee and snacks on your behalf. Thank-you for your goodwill.
