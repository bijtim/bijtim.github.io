Vovito : A Family Run Coffee Shop

Seattleites take their coffee seriously and that’s why we, at Vovito, offer the best locally roasted specialty coffee beans. Our roasters travel the world seeking out the rarest, most flavorful coffee beans. These specialty beans are ethically sourced and come as direct trade from coffee farmers, so you’ll experience exquisite coffee tastes from across the globe right in your cup. So please, open the bag and share our passion for the Bean! Enjoy

History of Vovito

Vovito, a cherished establishment renowned for its unique combination of coffee, pizza, and gelato, had long been a fixture in the bustling heart of Downtown Seattle and the vibrant community of Bellevue. Their cafes were cherished destinations for food enthusiasts seeking a blend of Italian flavors. However, in 2021, Vovito made the bold decision to shift their business entirely online, adapting to the changing landscape of the culinary industry. Though their physical presence in these two cities was no more, Vovito's commitment to serving exceptional coffee lived on in the digital realm, allowing patrons to continue enjoying their delightful offerings from the comfort of their own homes.
