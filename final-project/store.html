Vovito : A Family Run Coffee Shop

Seattleites take their coffee seriously and that’s why we, at Vovito, use only the best locally roasted specialty coffee beans. These single origin and organic beans come as Fair Trade from various regions along the coffee belt, so you’ll experience exquisite coffee tastes from across the globe right in your cup. We pull our shots on amazing La Marzocco machine that focus on precision of extraction and espresso profiling. We offer a variety of milk and milk alternatives (Almond, Coconut, Hemp, Oat and Soy) and numerous flavor choices so you can enjoy your coffee exactly the way you like it. Transform your latte into a mocha with white or dark chocolate, or rediscover our classic cappuccino or indulge in a smooth, caffeine packed experience with a Nitro Cold Brew. Your drink is waiting to be discovered at Vovito – so come on in! Cin Cin!
